Services

ASI offers several options in receiving interior design services. Below is an outline of services offered. I try to meet everyone's needs and comfort levels in achieving their dream space.

Interior Design Consultation

This is a one-time fee of $125 for up to 2 hour consultation. The consultation includes, but is not limited to, suggestions for furniture placement, paint colors, window treatments, future purchases, accessories, fabrics, lighting and construction.

Additional Hourly Services

Additional hourly services and/or consultations are charged at an hourly rate of $125 per hour. For example, to source fabric for window treatments, look for lighting options, meetings with contractors, etc.

On-line Design Service

If you live outside the Richmond area (or prefer to handle everything online) we offer an online decorating service. You send photos and measurements of the room and anything you would like to remain in the room. I will provide you with suggested furniture placement, color options, fabrics, accessories, lighting etc.(a sort of shopping list….all available online) and you will implement these items in your own space. The fee for online design is $375 per room. ASI reserves the right to implement an additional charge for large spaces or those which may be considered two rooms.
