Welcome To Amy Spearing Interiors

I am a wife, a mother of three, and a self-proclaimed Francophile passionate about bringing beautiful design to all people…not just those with large budgets. I received my degree in Pre-Law from the University of Central Florida and later my Interior Design Certification from the University of Richmond.

I believe interior design is only successful when the space reflects the people who live there. Part of what I do includes using what the client already has and loves. My goal is to create an extraordinary space that is both beautiful and usable. A good designer will listen to the clients' needs and style and make their vision a reality. Know More
